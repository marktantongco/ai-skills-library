# Social Content Pillars

## Context

Use this skill to generate monthly content calendars, content strategies, or post templates for social media across one or more brands/platforms. Covers pillar definition, platform formatting, captions, and scheduling.

**Trigger phrases:** "content calendar," "social media strategy," "content pillars," "monthly posts," "social media templates."

---

## Instructions

### Step 1: Define Brand Voice

1. **Per brand document:** name, industry, target audience (age, interests, platforms), 3 voice traits (from: professional, casual, witty, educational, inspiring, provocative, empathetic, authoritative), topics to avoid, visual style.
2. **Voice reference card:** "[Brand] sounds like [person] talking about [topic]. We always: [2 rules]. We never: [2 rules]. Example: '...'"

### Step 2: Set Content Pillars

3. **Define 3-5 pillars**, each with: name, description, percentage allocation (sums to 100%), 3-5 topic ideas.
   ```
   Example: Educational Tips (40%), Industry Insights (25%), Behind the Scenes (20%), Social Proof (15%)
   ```
4. **Map pillars to platforms:** Instagram (visual/Reels), Twitter/X (timely/threads), LinkedIn (long-form/professional), TikTok (trending/personality), YouTube (deep dives).

### Step 3: Generate Monthly Calendar

5. **30-day table:** Date | Day | Platform | Pillar | Format | Topic | Status.
6. **Frequency:** Instagram 4-5/week, Twitter 1-3/day, LinkedIn 3-5/week, TikTok 3-5/week, YouTube 1-2/week.
7. **Batch by day:** Monday=educational, Wednesday=insights, Friday=personality.

### Step 4: Post Templates

8. **Caption structures per pillar:**
   - **Educational:** Hook (question/bold claim) → Context → Numbered tips (3-5) → CTA
   - **Industry Insight:** News reference → Your take (2-3 sentences) → Implication → Question
   - **Social Proof:** Result headline with numbers → Brief story → Lesson → CTA
9. **Hashtag strategy:** Instagram 20-30 (niche/medium/broad mix), Twitter 1-3, LinkedIn 3-5, TikTok 3-5.
10. **Engagement hooks** (rotate, no repeats within 2 weeks):
    - "Most people get [topic] wrong. Here's what works:"
    - "I spent [time] learning [topic]. Summary:"
    - "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]"
    - "The #1 thing I wish I knew about [topic]:"
    - "[Number] [resources] that changed how I [verb] [topic]"

### Step 5: Repurposing & Scheduling

11. **Repurposing matrix:** LinkedIn article → Twitter thread → Instagram carousel → TikTok talking-head → YouTube deep dive. One piece of content, five platforms.
12. **Scheduling:** Batch-create weekly (Sun/Mon), schedule 1-2 weeks ahead, leave 20% slots open for reactive content.

---

## Constraints

- NEVER post identical captions across platforms. Adapt tone and length per platform.
- NEVER use > 2 emojis per line.
- NEVER buy followers or use engagement bait ("comment YES").
- Pillars MUST be defined before calendar generation.
- Every post MUST have a clear purpose (educate, engage, convert, or build awareness).
- Brand voice MUST stay consistent across platforms.
- Always include a CTA (even "save this" or "share with someone who needs this").
- No links in Instagram captions; no external links in LinkedIn post body.

## Examples

### Example 1: SaaS Startup (B2B)

```
Brand: CloudMetrics (analytics platform)
Voice: Authoritative + Educational + Witty | Audience: CTOs, engineering managers
Pillars: Data Insights (40%), Product Tips (30%), Engineering Culture (20%), Customer Stories (10%)
Weekly: Mon=Insight (LinkedIn+Twitter thread), Tue=Tip (carousel+tweet), Thu=Culture (text+hot take), Fri=Roundup
```

### Example 2: Personal Creator Brand

```
Brand: @designbytes (UX designer, 25K followers)
Voice: Casual + Educational + Inspiring | Audience: Junior designers, PMs (22-35)
Pillars: Design Tutorials (45%), Career Advice (25%), Design Critiques (20%), Personal Journey (10%)
Repurpose: Figma tutorial → YouTube video → Instagram Reel (60s) → Twitter thread (5 steps) → LinkedIn carousel
```
